The Accomodations at La Frontera

We stayed at La Frontera, on a large piece of private equestrian property. It was perfect for astro-photography purposes. In fact, it exceeds anything we could have dreamt. They had 4 cabins which could each accommodate parties between 2 to 8 people. All are relatively new and pleasantly decorated, complete with separate kitchen, dining, living, and bedroom areas. We stayed in a 2-bedroom cabana designed for 6 people. There is plenty of water with nice streams & rivers for a tranquil getaway. With no cell phone service and no telephones, it seemed we were finally able escape civilization. Of course, the sky was crystal clear and the seeing was excellent.
